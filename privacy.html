🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: 08/06/2025

By using this bot, you agree to the terms below. We respect your privacy and ensure the conscious and secure use of your data.

📦 What data we collect:

  • User and server IDs
  • Messages sent only in channels configured for translation
  • Commands used with the bot

📈 How we use this data:

  • To process and provide requested translations
  • To save specific server and channel settings
  • To improve overall performance and functionality of the bot
  • Translation Cache:
    We keep translations cached to reduce costs. If an identical message has already been translated, the response will be reused — saving resources and speeding up replies.

🔐 Sharing and Security:

  • We never share data with third parties.
  • Never share your passwords or personal data with anyone.
  • No personal data, passwords, or private content is stored or used.
  • We take measures to ensure the security and protection of information.

🧾 Your rights:
